Around is truly multipurpose template designed to facilitate the needs of businesses from different niches. It offers demos for creative businesses as well as corporate segment and startups. Around offers the collection of all necessary secondary pages for you to build fully-featured modern website. We also included designs for Blog, E-commerce (shop) and Help Center to meet all the needs you may have. For those who are looking to build Web application with enabled registration and user account functionalities Around has well-designed User Account & Dashboard screens.
